ALCHEMY: Once you enter the MEDIUM, the PUNCH DESIGNIX is added to the PHERNALIA REGISTRY. It costs 4 of the CLIENT'S PRIMARY GRIST to place. Notice that on the back of your SYLLADEX CARDS, a CAPTCHA CODE is provided for each item. You may type in the CAPTCHA CODE at the PUNCH DESIGNIX to punch its equivalent onto a SYLLADEX CARD. By putting 2 different PUNCHED CARDS into the TOTEM LATHE, it will produce a CARVED CRUXITE DOWEL which combines the two PUNCHED CARDS and you may then proceed to create the item with the ALCHEMITER.
